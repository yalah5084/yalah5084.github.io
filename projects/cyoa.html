Choose your own adventure project

Context

The CYOA project is to practice collaboration and linking files. The groups were selected based on our top 3 choices in a form to choose what we're most interested in. All of the choices in the form were created by us.

Content

My group and I made an adventure on paranormal activities and since we are three people in the group, we created 3 doors. Each door has it's own story and different decisions to makes, some decisions can lead to the end of the game and others can lead to a key to another door or an exit.

Reflection

I really enjoyed making this project and it's one of my favorite projects I've done because it was really fun making the plan with my group and let our creativity run. Thanks to the many challenges, I learned how to put block elements next to each other like the 3 doors with the images, I learned how to change the color of a text when hovered over and when it's linked since the a element automatically changes the text to blue. With this project, my SEP notes came in handy to make the html more appealing with the colors, line height and margin.
